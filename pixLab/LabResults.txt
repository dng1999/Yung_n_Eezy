Questions (pg 4)
1. How can you make pink? 
    r=255, g=0, b=255
2. How can you make yellow?
    r=255, g=255, b=51
3. How can you make purple?
    r=153, g=0, b=255
4. How can you make white?
    r=255, g=255, b=255
5. How can you make dark gray?
    r=102, g=102, b=102
